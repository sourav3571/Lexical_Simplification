Lexical Simplification Pipeline

A high-precision, modular system combining deep learning (BERT), semantic models (WordNet), and word association spaces (GloVe) to make text easy to read.

📥 Raw Input Sentence (e.g., "The results were ambiguous.")
Stage 1 preprocessing.py

Text Preprocessing

Cleans, parses, and identifies the structural parts of the sentence using spaCy.

  • Tokenization: Splits the raw sentence into individual word units.
  • POS Tagging: Marks parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives, etc.).
  • Lemmatization: Extracts root words (e.g., "running" becomes "run").
  • Filtering: Ignores grammar tokens like prepositions and punctuation early to save computer resources.
Content Tokens with POS Tags
Stage 2 contextual_cwi.py

Complex Word Identification (CWI)

Evaluates each word to decide if it is too complex for an average reader.

  • Zipf Frequency: Rarity rating in human language (lower scores = rarer words).
  • BERT Surprisal: Gauges how unexpected a word is in its local sentence context.
  • Familiarity Check: Compares words against known basic word lists (Oxford 3000, Dale-Chall).
  • Morphological Size: Measures character length and spoken syllable count.
❓ Is the word complex?
Yes, Target Word Selected (e.g., "ambiguous")
Stage 3 word_sense_disambiguation.py

Word Sense Disambiguation (WSD)

Finds the exact dictionary meaning of the complex word based on the sentence context.

  • WordNet Lookup: Pulls all potential meanings (synsets) of the word.
  • BERT Embeddings: Encodes the sentence and WordNet definition sentences into context vectors.
  • Cosine Similarity: Matches definitions mathematically; the highest matching sense is selected.
True Word Sense definition
Stage 4 candidate_generator.py

Candidate Generation & Filtering

Brainstorms alternative words and removes options that are invalid or too difficult.

  • Multi-Source Generation: Gathers words from WordNet relations, GloVe context neighbors, and BERT fill-in-the-blank guesses.
  • Part-of-Speech Matching: Retains only words that match the target's grammar category (e.g., only adjectives).
  • Simplicity Gating: Discards candidate words that are harder or rarer than the original word.
Simpler Candidate List (e.g., ["unclear", "vague"])
Stage 5 model.py

Contextual Neural Ranking

Evaluates and ranks the candidate synonyms using a custom-trained Gated Fusion Network.

  • Semantic Fit Sub-Network: Scores context match (BERT), grammar probability (MLM), and dictionary alignment (WordNet similarity).
  • Simplicity Fit Sub-Network: Scores readable gains (character reduction, syllable reduction, frequency increase).
  • Multi-Gated Fusion: Score = Semantic_Fit × Simplicity_Fit. The highest-scoring word wins.
Winner Selected (e.g., "unclear")
Stage 6 inference.py

Grammatical Substitution

Safely inflects the winning candidate and inserts it into the sentence.

  • Inflection: Conjugates the word (e.g., past/present, singular/plural, capitalization) to fit grammatically.
  • Offset Adjustments: Safely aligns index positions to allow sequential multi-word replacements in the same sentence.
🎉 Simplified Output Sentence (e.g., "The results were unclear.")
